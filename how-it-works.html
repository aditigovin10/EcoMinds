Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use EcoMinds tutoring?

Our live tutoring is designed for middle and high school students interested in climate and environmental science.

Who are the tutors?

Tutors are trained high school students who have already studied these topics and want to help others learn.

Is this free?

Yes! EcoMinds tutoring is completely free and community-driven.

What subjects are covered?

We cover climate science, ecosystems, renewable energy, fossil fuels, weathering, and more.
