How Live Tutoring Works

No scheduling, no pressure — just join, ask questions, and leave whenever you want.

Nervous to talk? You can type questions in chat — many students do!

1

Pick a Topic
Weathering, fossil fuels, ecosystems, renewable energy, and more.
Choose what you're currently learning in class.

2

Join a Peer Session
Get help from trained high school tutors.
You can keep camera off and just use chat if you prefer.

3

Learn & Track Progress
Review notes or mini quizzes to reinforce learning.
Everything stays saved so you can come back later.

Before you join

  • Have your homework or question ready
  • Bring a notebook or open a doc
  • Join the Zoom link

All sessions are moderated and focused only on school learning.
