By students, for students, for Earth.

EcoMinds is a student-led learning platform where high schoolers teach climate and environmental science through tutoring, lessons, and shared resources.

From oceans to ecosystems, learn the science that shapes our planet!

Whether you're curious about ecosystems, oceans, or how climate works, our peer-led lessons make learning fun and interactive. Jump in, ask questions, and see how science shapes our planet every day!

Tools, resources, and oppurtunities to explore and make a difference!

Access a variety of curated resources to deepen your understanding of enviornmental and climate science. From articles and videos, to simulated projects, everything is designed to help students learn and grow. Plus, discover virtual volunteer oppurtunites for students in grades 9-12 so you can apply your knowledge and take action for the planet.
